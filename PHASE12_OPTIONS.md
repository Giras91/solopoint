## Phase 12 Option A: Profit Analytics & Cost Tracking ‚≠ê

**Focus:** Transform SoloPoint into a business intelligence tool for profitability analysis

### Features:
1. **Product Cost Management**
   - Set product cost (COGS) per unit
   - Track cost variance per variant
   - Bulk cost import/update

2. **Profit Margin Calculations**
   - Real-time profit calculation per order
   - Profit margin % by product/category
   - Gross margin vs net margin tracking

3. **Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
   - Profit trend chart (daily/weekly/monthly)
   - Profit by category breakdown
   - Product profitability ranking
   - Cost vs revenue comparison
   - Break-even analysis

4. **Profit Reports**
   - Daily profit summary
   - Category profitability report
   - Product profitability ranking
   - Cost variance analysis
   - PDF export for financial review

5. **Inventory Cost Valuation**
   - Stock value by FIFO/LIFO methods
   - Inventory carrying cost
   - Cost of goods sold (COGS) tracking

### Why This Matters:
- Business owners care most about **profit, not just revenue**
- Essential for pricing strategy optimization
- Helps identify loss-making products/categories
- Supports financial reporting for taxes/audits

### Effort: Medium (3-4 days)
### Complexity: Medium-High

2. **Order Status Management**
   - Start/pause/complete order
   - Mark items as ready

   - Pre-order/future order support

3. **Chef Interface**
   - Simplified UI for kitchen operations
   - Large fonts for visibility
   - Audio/visual alerts for new orders
   - Filter by item type

4. **Integration**
   - Auto-sync between POS and kitchen

### Why This Matters:
